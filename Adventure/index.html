Arriving at the Grand Canyon

September 21, 2020

I started the day at home in my bed, and I ended the day at a hotel overlooks the Grand Canyon. I went in a limo to the airport so I could look for the secret treasure in the Grand Canyon. I got to my hotel, which was named "Grand Canyon Village", around 5:00 PM and here I am now. This is a list of important stuff they told me to bring: water, food, treasure clues, sunscreen, and my swimsuit. The treasure clues were found in the envelope sent to me. It's been a crazy day, but I only know one thing. I am looking for treasure. They call this the "Gateway to wonder", so it must it's a gateway to the treasure.

Looking for Clues

September 22, 2020

I woke up today with a mission. The first clue said, "The noise that monkeys make will lead you on." I did some research and it turns out there is an "Ooh Aah" trail in the Canyon. On my way there I crossed a skywalk called "The Grand Canyon Skywalk". I strongly believe in the saying, "Always take the scenic route," which means always go the way that will give you the best views. I hiked into the Canyon and I got to Ooh Aah point. One the back of the sign it said, "Water throughout the fall will cause you to find what you are looking for." I realized that the treasure must be behind the waterfall. It was getting late, so I had to go the next day.
